<h2>About NanoCMS v0.1</h2>
<p>
	Nano CMS is the tinyest CMS you can find around or the tinyest that i could have made :)<br>
	The user interface and the functionality are very very simple and easy to use.<br><br>
</p>
<p>
Nano cms is simple and neat enough to manage websites with static content through an easy to use interface.<br><br>

Through this CMS you can create pages without actually touching any code.<br><br>

You can manage website pages very easily and the links in the sidebar are generated automatically...<br>

If you dont like the design it is very easy to integrate new designs also...
</p>

<h2>Features up in v0.1</h2>
Some of the features go this way...
<ul>
 <li>Easy integration</li>
 <li>Simple ( or simplest ) interface</li>
 <li>Links for the sidebar are generated Automatically!!</li>
 <li>Manage pages in sidebar</li>
 <li>Add pages other than sidebar pages</li>
 <li>Filebased - no databases blah</li>
 <li>Best suitable for sites with only static content</li>
</ul>
